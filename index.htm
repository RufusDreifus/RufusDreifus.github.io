👋 Hello everyone

My name is Nikolay Bukharin. I’m a fluid mechanics researcher from Calgary, Canada 🇨🇦. I earned my MSc in Applied Mathematics from Moscow State University and a PhD in Fluid Mechanics at the University of Calgary, where I also completed two postdocs.

I teach a broad range of engineering subjects at The Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT) (current) and have taught at the University of Calgary (2009, 2012, 2014).

I’ve also worked on R&D in heat and mass transfer, instrumentation, and experimental fluid mechanics — including Binary Fluid Ejector (BFE), Bluff Body Aerodynamics & Active Wake Control, and Waste Heat, among others.
